About Us

Starting from 2017 Lead & Follow Dance School is teaching people how to dance with great success and because of our innovative methods and unique studio format we are strongly presented among the leaders in the market of dance related services

  • Group classes

    If you like to practice in a group or want to find new friends and have fun, look at our group classes. Try Swing, Tango, Salsa, and more! Everyone is welcome—just look for the beginner, advanced, and all labels in the schedule to make sure you feel comfortable during the class and get joy, not the achy feet. To start, choose your dance style—Social Dance, International System, or American System. We also have a program for Kids

    Private classes offer the most in-depth and individual approach to your learning. Your PERSONAL TRAINER will help you set and achieve goals and will develop a personalized plan for each of your classes. Make your UNIQUE WAY through the ballroom! Getting married?     Workshops, themed parties, dance nights, and other events are an important part of our school's life. Bring everything you've learned to the dance floor and meet other students. We have the most welcoming atmosphere—at dance events, even the shiest of our students let themselves go.
